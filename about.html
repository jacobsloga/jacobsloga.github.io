What is Jacob's Loga?


(Jacob's Ladder meets logarithmic growth)

Jacob's Loga aims to show the correlation between wealth & gaining more wealth (as well as everything that comes from that). The name is a combination of Jacob's Ladder, where teams must work together to climb onto each level, and logarithmic growth, where the further you progress the easier a challenge gets.
